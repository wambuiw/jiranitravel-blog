48 Hours in Nairobi for First-Time Visitors

Posted on June 25, 2025 | By Wambui Lucy

Day 1 – Culture, Wildlife & Matatus

Begin your Nairobi adventure with breakfast at Java House, then head to the Giraffe Centre. For a deeper dive, Nairobi National Museum is just minutes away. Grab a matatu to Kenyatta Market for nyama choma and music.

Day 2 – Urban Vibes & Nature Escapes

Visit Karura Forest for a morning walk. Explore the Maasai Market for crafts and souvenirs. Wind down with sunset at The Alchemist, a local favorite for music, food, and culture.

Local Tips

  • Use Bolt or Uber to get around safely
  • Dress modestly when heading to markets
  • Try local dishes like ugali, sukuma, and pilau
